How to study smarter and not harder!

Studying can be very challenging at times( almost everytime ),buuut by using the right study technique for each subject learning can get pretty fun!

Which is why you'll find in this website all the study techniques you'll need to use to ace each subject

Math

The best way to study math is through consistent, active practice rather than passive reading, focusing on understanding concepts, and working through numerous problems

English

Effective English study methods involve daily immersion and active practice: watching films with subtitles, reading, listening to podcasts, and speaking aloud, even to oneself.

Science

To master science, focus on active recall and practice problems rather than just rereading. Use the Feynman Technique by explaining concepts in simple terms to spot knowledge gaps, and apply spaced repetition to lock facts into your long-term memory.

History

To master history, focus on cause and effect rather than just dates. Use timelines to visualize sequences and narrative practice to explain why events happened, turning facts into a story.
